Liquidation & Closeouts

Liquidity helps a company pay off short-term debts, and cover expenses. If you're closing out or liquidating, get in touch and we can help you with buying most of your stock.

Outdated Stock

Companies can get good value for their non-moving stocks. Non-moving stock refers to products that a company is unable to sell. We purchase non-moving stock and export it.

Rejected Stock

Every so often certain products in your stock are unapproved for sale in the UAE and GCC region for various reasons including security, quality or safety concerns. We can take that stock off of your hands.

Surplus Stock

If you're a company that frequently takes on massive projects, chances are at completion, you'll have tons of material left which you can neither sell, nor use. We can step in and purchase that material.

Free Shelf Space

Storing a large quantity of non-moving, useless stock can be very expensive, especially in the GCC region. We purchase that stock and help you free up your shelf space, and your cash.

Recycling
