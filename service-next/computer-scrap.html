Thing to know before selling your scrap.

Is there any scrap value in old computers?

The value of old computers depends on their condition and what type of components they have. Some people may be willing to pay for certain parts, but it is generally not worth trying to sell an old computer as a whole. The scrap value of old computers is increasing. This is because the technology that was used to make them has been replaced by newer and better technologies.

What parts of an old computer are valuable?

Outdated laptops may be filled with priceless metals like gold, copper, and aluminum. Additionally, you can market and sell motherboards, RAM, CPUs, PCI, gold fingers, and other gold-plated components which could be of quite some value to you.

How much is a laptop worth in scrap metal?

The abundance of valuable metals found in the bulkier, older laptops is not present in the sleek, contemporary models, yet they can still be profitable.

The average price of a scrap laptop is about 0.51 USD/LB, but it could vary depending on the quality and company, ranging from 0.25 USD/LB to 1.25 USD/LB.
